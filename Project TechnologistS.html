Project technologist

Location: Squamish, BC - Must reside or be willing to relocate

Employment Type: Full-Time / Contract (Flexible)

Company: Acadia Engineering

About Us

Acadia Engineering is a growing consulting firm specializing in civil engineering, water resources, and land development. With projects across Vancouver Island, the Lower Mainland, and the Sea-to-Sky region, we are committed to delivering innovative and sustainable solutions for our clients.

We are currently seeking a Project Technologist to join our team in Squamish, BC. This role offers the opportunity to work on exciting projects in land development, infrastructure, and municipal engineering while enjoying the outdoor lifestyle of the Sea-to-Sky corridor.

What You’ll Do
  • Assist in the design, drafting, and preparation of engineering drawings using AutoCAD Civil 3D.
  • Conduct site inspections, surveys, and data collection for civil engineering projects.
  • Support project coordination, permitting, and liaising with municipalities and stakeholders.
  • Perform quantity take-offs, cost estimates, and assist with tender preparation.
  • Contribute to stormwater management, grading plans, and erosion control designs.
  • Collaborate with engineers, clients, and contractors to ensure project success.
What We’re Looking For
  • Individual working towards their P. L. Eng. designation
  • Computer Language skills
  • Diploma in Civil Engineering Technology or a related field.
  • Minimum 2 years of relevant experience in civil engineering, land development, or infrastructure projects.
  • Proficiency in AutoCAD Civil 3D (experience with GIS or Bluebeam is a plus).
  • Strong understanding of BC municipal standards and permitting processes.
  • Excellent problem-solving, organizational, and communication skills.
  • Ability to work independently and as part of a team
  • Valid Class 5 BC driver’s license and willingness to travel for site visits.
What We Offer
  • Competitive salary based on experience.
  • Remote work arrangements during certain times of the year. Anywhere on the planet with a reliable internet connection.
  • Opportunity to work on diverse and meaningful projects.
  • Professional development and training support.
  • A dynamic and supportive team culture.
How to Apply

Send your resume and a brief cover letter to Josh@acadiaeng.ca with the subject line "Project Technologist - Squamish Application."

Applications will be reviewed on a rolling basis. We thank all applicants for their interest; however, only those selected for an interview will be contacted.
