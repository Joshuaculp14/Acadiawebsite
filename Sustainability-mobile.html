WHAT WE STAND FOR

environmental impact

Acadia is committed to:

  • Achieving and maintaining carbon neutrality in all operations.
  • Demonstrating environmental stewardship through responsible resource management and waste reduction.
  • Incorporating sustainable design principles to minimize environmental impact.
  • Collaborating with local communities and stakeholders to ensure environmental solutions are practical, impactful, and forward-thinking.
  • Working with First Nations to protect our environment. 

Sustainability in engineering

We are dedicated to:

  • Implementing Low Impact Development (LID) to reduce environmental footprints.
  • Prioritizing eco-conscious design for long-term environmental stewardship.
  • Engineering climate-resilient infrastructure to withstand future challenges
  • Leveraging digital twin technology for smarter, data-driven solutions.
  • Encouraging active transportation to foster healthier, greener communities.
