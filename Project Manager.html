Civil Engineer / Project Manager

Location: Belleville, ON - Must reside or be willing to relocate

Employment Type: Full-Time / Contract (Flexible)

Company: Acadia Engineering

Acadia Engineering Inc. is a multi-disciplinary company that offers engineering services including subdivision development designs and municipal infrastructure designs. 

Acadia is seeking a Civil Engineer / Project Manager with 5+ years of engineering experience. You will be responsible for leading development and municipal project designs, using Ontario Standards, with minimal supervision. You will meet manage projects with minimal supervision. 

Key Responsibilities:

  • Overseeing detailed design for subdivision and site plan projects
  • Overseeing detailed design for municipal infrastructure projects
  • Overseeing detailed cost estimates
  • Overseeing detailed tender packages
  • Drafting Proposals

Supplementary Responsibilities: 

  • Field Inspections
  • Surveying

Required Qualifications: 

  • Minimum five (5) years Project Management experience
  • Excellent with Ontario Regulations
  • Excellent oral, written, and interpersonal skills
  • Valid driver's license and working vehicle

Supplementary Assets: 

  • Civil Engineering Certification (C.Tech, CET, EIT, or P.Eng.)
  • Inspection experience
  • Project management experience
  • Understanding of land development process
  • Watermodeling (Infowater, Epanet, WaterCAD)
  • Stormwater management (HMS, Otthymo)
  • Structural experience

Submission can be made to: Steve@acadiaeng.ca
